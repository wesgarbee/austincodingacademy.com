Tuition Questions

Each 8-week class is $2,990, and each track consists of 4 classes.
We encourage you to check with your current employer to see if they’d be willing to assist with the costs. Also, we partner with a reputable organization for financing.
We occasionally offer scholarships! Make sure you’re signed up for our blog and are subscribed to our newsletter to learn about these opportunities as they arise. Also note that if you’re a driver for Lyft, we offer tuition discounts - click here to learn more.
We do not offer financial aid.
